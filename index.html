Web Development

Web development is the process of creating websites and web applications that people can access on the internet. It includes everything from designing the look of a website to ensuring it works properly and runs smoothly. Think of it like building a houseâ€”where web developers are the architects and builders who create a space for your business online.

1. Front-End Development (UI/UX Design)

  • This is what users see and interact with on a website.
  • Example Technologies: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, React.js, EJS

2. Back-End Development (Server & Database Management)

  • Handles the logic, database, and server-side operations.
  • Example Technologies: Node.js, PHP, MySQL, MongoDB, Express.js

3. Full-Stack Development

  • A combination of front-end and back-end development.
  • Example Technologies: MEAN Stack (MongoDB, Express.js, Angular, Node.js), MERN Stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js)

4. Web Hosting & Management

  • Hosting the website on a server and managing its performance and security.
  • Example Services: Hostinger, Bluehost, AWS, cPanel

5. E-Commerce Development

  • Building online stores for businesses to sell products.
  • Example Platforms: Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento
